CINEPATH


VISUALIZAED INTERFACE FOR CINEMATIC FILM LOCATIONS


My personal voyage of cinema in terms of visualizing the actual filming locations scavenged on the Internet sources including IMDb, articles and Google Maps Street View. I worked independently to design and prototype the platform using JavaScript and Google Maps API under the advice from professor Ben Grosser in New Media Department, UIUC.


Click on title or photo to see it in action.
