CINEPATH

Cinepath is a visualized interface for cinematic film locations. This is a personal voyage of cinema in terms of the interdisciplinary studies with cinema and data visualization.


GITHUB PARIS, TEXAS FRANCES HA

Paris, Texas, Directed by Wim Wender, 1984

DEFINE THE PROBLEM

It starts with my obsession with maps and cinema... What are the filming locations for one film? What transportations are being used in the film? And are there any patterns comparing different directors?

IDEATION

I was inspired by a YouTube Video which mapped the opening scene of Baby Drive. However, instead of making a video with tools much as Adobe After Effects to animate all the routes, I had this visualization automaton in mind which uses webpages as the medium and allows user interactions to explore the map online.

CHALLENGES

There are mainly two challenges in this project:
      1. Data Collection
      2. Implementation

PROTOTYPE

IMPROVEMENTS

To further understand a director's film locations throughout their career, more data visualization could be completed on one film director, to better analyze their moving patterns, interests of certain regions and transportation perferences.

Technically, the data collection process could also be optimized with deep-learning programs to recognize locations by video processing to extract frames in the film, In this way, it could reduce the time and efforts to generate more visualizations with the utilization of a back-end database.
