LILAN FILM CODING PLACES

Lilan Yang is an artist, cinéphile, and flâneuse whose practice involves both digital and analog media such as computing, data visualization, machine learning, photography, and filmmaking. Her artistic practice focuses on fiction/reality in cinema, how it intertwines with our memories and shared mentality and filming and making 16mm experimental short films. She holds a BS in Computer Engineering from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Currently, she is living with a golden retriever puppy called Ollie and working towards an MFA in Digital + Media at Rhode Island School of Design.
