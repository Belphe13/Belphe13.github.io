K-12 LEARNING LAB


I worked on the research team that developed the first free module of K-12 professional development course that helps K-12 educators integrate design thinking into the classroom from May 2020 to August 2020.


PROBLEM

Illinois educators within the K-12 public education system have limited and inconsistent teacher training in human-centered design using research-based design thinking strategies.


MISSION

To Provide K-12 educators in the State of Illinois with the highest quality human-centered design education and training through research-based content, multimodal service delivery and strong team leadership.


MY RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Secondary researches of competitive programs nationwide and online learning platforms
  • Recruited K-12 educators and program directors using surveys and interviews
  • Coordinated within university system for platform integration


PROCESS


RESULTS

I reached out to people who filled out the survey and conducted 30-min interviews with them to better understand user needs to develop our first module.


NEXT STEPS

The team will bring our contents produced from the university media studio online as asynchronous modules and pilot our first part of the training series on thinkific in fall 2020. And the team will continue to develop the second and third part of the course to be synchronous group sessions.
