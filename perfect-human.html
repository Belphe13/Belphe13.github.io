THE PERFECT HUMAN

Through the process similar to a previous coding project CINEML which takes machine learning approach to analyze cinema, I looked into a 13-min short Black and White film The Perfect Human by JÃ¸rgen Leth and remade the cult classic with generative adversarial network (GAN), which was later transformed into analog form, reexamining and questioning the contested notion of perfect human.




WHY PERFECT HUMAN?

To some extent, every film is staged. The original film of The Perfect Human by Leth was too obviously staged in a boundless space. It is an unrealistic environment to portray the non-existing perfect human. White background successfully situates the concept of perfect human far from the reality.

The reason I intentionally choose the 360p low-resolution video found on YouTube and train a neural network based on it is to create an ambiguous and abstract remake that complicates the contested notion of perfect human. The computer-generated fakes also played a crucial part in generalizing what perfect human means.
