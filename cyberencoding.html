INSPIRATION

Based on previous p5.js practices Breath of Despair and Moment of Desperation, 2 software mirror which allows camera-based interactions by pixelizing the captured images, this project imports film clips as recreations with primitive shapes to generate new meanings and aesthetics. My intention is to find a trio of classical sci-fi movies in the 20th century that are interconnected but fascinating in their own ways.

2001: STARGATE







The first featured clip comes for the renowed 2001: A Space Odyssey directed by Stanley Kubrick.




STALKER: RAILROAD







If I'll have to name another sci-fi as significant as 2001 in the world of cinema in the 70s, Solaris directed by Andrei Tarkovsky has to be nominated with no hesitation. Instead of the much similar journey into the space, my focus has been shifted to the exploration on the earth.
