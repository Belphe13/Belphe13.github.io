DROPPER


Dropper is a music listening software that builds a listening community that encourages users to share what they are listening to associated with geographic locations. Users can visualize their own music journey on a map, while exploring new places with music other people share. This user interface design project was made possible with Adobe XD.




DESIGN CHALLENGE

Music has always been a carrier of culture that can be timeless and transcendental, and the music listening experience is no longer limited to crowded concerts or stationarily through hi-fi systems. Oftentimes people are on the move when they listen to music, on their daily commute, on road trips and exploring around the world. These individual memories associated with music and places can be collective too. What are ways we can better preserve these precious moments in life and revisit these moments through listening?

Thus I propose Dropper, a location-based music app, is to combine these two concepts of geolocation and music together, and create new experiences.
