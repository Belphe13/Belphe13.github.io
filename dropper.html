DROPPER


The fascination of how people store and retrieve geolocation data helps with a better understanding of our surroundings, and the landscape that influences people in generations. And music also plays an important part to help shape the place. The main idea of DROPPER, a location-based music app, is to combine these two concepts of geolocation and music together to create new experiences.
