OLD SCHOOL


This is a semiotic gestural concept design that simulates physical music control interfaces, allowing users to play/pause/shuffle the music using their hands without interacting with a physical interface.



PROBLEM

How can you control your music playback while working on other tasks? How do you save time from switching to another software or application on your digital devices? We, a team of two designers, suggest a semiotic gestural design that controls the music playback.


RESPONSIBILITIES

I collaborated with a graphic designer to design a nonverbal gestural interface. After brainstorming the concept based on the interface of the digital amplifiers and turntables. I mainly worked on the photo and video production side of the project, directing and producing the wizard-of-oz videos showing the gestures in action in public and private settings.
