CINEMASK

Inspired by two of the P5.JS PRACTICES, Cinemask is another attempt of video processing with pixel manipulations.


GITHUB

Screenshot

DEFINE THE PROBLEM

I don't really see the problem here. It is a showing of appreciation towards cinema in different forms. Can the recreation of film footages become nothing other than visual appeal?

IDEATION

Based on previous p5.js practices BREATH OF DESPAIR and MOMENT OF DESPERATION, 2 software mirror which allows camera-based interactions by pixelizing the captured images, this project imports film clips as recreations with primitive shapes to generate new meanings and aesthetics. My intention is to find a trio of classical sci-fi movies in the 20th century that are interconnected but fascinating in their own ways.

CHALLENGES

Besides the time-consuming part (not really a chanllenge) is to not using regular video processing tool such as MATLAB for I really wanted to use the decoration stretch function and the online p5.js web editor cannot hold up large files, most of the time on the project was spent on setting up the local server. And instead of directly exporting the canvas/video, I chose the simple screenshot to get the job done.
