TIMELINE


SCD has been tasked to use a human-centered approach to explore and understand the options for creating engagement opportunities at Grainger College of Engineering in a COVID-19 world.

After the kickoff meeting over Zoom, we drafted a research proposal that presented to stakeholders and quickly dived into the user research recruitment. We conducted one-hour-long interview sessions with students that have experience online learning to get the student perspectives. And we identified several opportunity areas and shared them back to participants and improved based on student feedbacks.
