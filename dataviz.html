How do mostly critiqued US Film Directors Do Through Market Feedback?

Given a meta dataset found online, I played around with Tableau as a data visualization tool. Filtering down to only consider US major film directors with at least 1,000 professional critic reviews in that dataset, I built two applied scatter charts not only about the average budget vs. gross for each director but also take into account Facebook likes for both cast and the movie itself. In addition to these scatter charts, user ratings on IMDb are presented in table with default color code of Tableau.
